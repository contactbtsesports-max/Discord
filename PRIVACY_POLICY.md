# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 13, 2026

## 1. Introduction

The Discord Auto-Moderation Bot ("Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and how we handle information.

## 2. Information We Collect

The Bot collects the following information:

- **Discord User IDs**: To identify users and apply moderation rules
- **Message Content**: To analyze and detect violations (profanity, insults, rule violations)
- **Server IDs**: To identify which Discord server the message came from
- **Timestamp Data**: To log when violations occurred

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Moderation Purposes**: To detect and block inappropriate content
- **Rule Enforcement**: To maintain server rules and community standards
- **Logging**: To maintain records of moderation actions
- **Service Improvement**: To improve the Bot's detection algorithms

## 4. Data Storage

- Message content is analyzed in real-time but not permanently stored
- Violation logs may be stored temporarily for server owners to review
- No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties

## 5. User Rights

You have the right to:

- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of the Bot's services
- Request information about what data we have about you

## 6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 7. Third-Party Services

The Bot may interact with Discord's API and services. Please refer to Discord's Privacy Policy for information about how Discord handles your data.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the repository.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through GitHub Issues.
